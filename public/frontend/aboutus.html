    We are a Bangladeshi law firm providing solution based legal service to our clients. Our firm, Law Wheel, is comprised of young, able and dynamic lawyers. They possess sound and practical command of legal knowledge. We aim to emphatically provide best-in-class and cost effective legal solutions to materialize our clients‚Äô objectives.

    The firm conducts extensive and up to the minute legal research in diverse, newly originated complex legal issues and presents their cohesive solutions. In dealing complex issues, we extensively examine the 200-year legal literature of the subcontinent, scrutinize reasoning of the both sides, formulate strategy and pursue them in court. In course of doing so, we employ classical and comprehensive legal resources embracing cutting-edge online legal resource of major legal systems of the world.

    Law Wheel is a criminal and constitutional law firm. Many a times, the counsels of the firm appeared in a number cases involving jurisprudential question of law and had the privilege of assisting the Highest Court in shaping jurisprudence in our legal system. In the same time, it has already garnered the reputation of dealing with civil and company matters adeptly. Especially, it has successfully served clients in the arena of pre-emption, title and ownership of land, formation of company and society, legal opinion, vetting, mutation, patent, copyright and trademark.

    The firm has the benchmark track record of handling cyber-crimes, financial crimes, prevention of human trafficking, domestic violence, offence relating to arms, explosive substances, anti-terrorism and anti- corruption matters both in Supreme Court and the District Courts.

    Law Wheel has been applauded for its contribution in solving complex constitutional and criminal legal issues. In the Apex Court of the country, the Counsels of the firm meaningfully contributed in interpreting constitution and statutes of the land and those cases have been reported in the prominent law reports of our jurisdiction. Furthermore, the adroitness of the firm in solution oriented, in-depth legal research has been appreciated in the leading national dailies of Bangladesh. The firm successfully represented its clients in Court in enforcing their fundamental rights against arbitrary exercise of power by the government, statutory bodies and autonomous institutions. In course of doing so, Law Wheel moved on behalf of citizens‚Äô of diverse backgrounds irrespective of race, sex, color and religion.

    In appropriate circumstances, Law Wheel is committed to provide pro bono legal service to the destitute and vulnerable class of the society and will strive to provide them with coveted remedies exploring the best relevant solutions.
